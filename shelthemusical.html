SHEL: A Historically Fictionalized Musical

SHEL told the story of Shel Silverstein from his own perspective. A central part of these designs was depicting Silverstein in his own hand. Emulating the style of his characters and lettering was an important part of indicating that this story was being told in his voice, the one he used in his poems and stories.

Through studying images of Silverstein's handwriting, I created a font that borrowed elements and quirks from his penmanship. This font was used in the marketing and promotional materials for the show's World Premier prouduction.
