Stranger Sings: The We-Thought-of-it-First Parody Musical

The client wanted their work to speak for itself, so this was almost entirely translating the design of the source material. While the Netflix show dealt with dark themes and imagery, this parody strayed far away from that tone, into the wild and silly. I felt that the more elements I could pull from the original, the more well primed the audience would be for that departure.
