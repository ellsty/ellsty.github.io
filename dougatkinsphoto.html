Doug Atkins Photography

I incorporated everything I could that the client felt was central to their process and method of working with their own clients. They wanted a simple and abstract mark, but wanted to reference a handful of memories that shaped them into the artist they are today, essentially featuring subtle easter eggs in the logo itself.

Themed around transformation and expression, the mark depicts natural sunlight passing though the lens of a camera, becoming something dynamic and new, and the secondary lighter color is a nod to the client's fond memory of dyeing their hair a vibrant platium blonde color.
